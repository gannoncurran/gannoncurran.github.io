Hi! I'm Gannon Curran. I'm a member of the June 2014 cohort of San Francisco's DevBootcamp. I've been a graphic designer is Seattle for — well — a long time. I've always liked programming, and sometimes I've had reasons to get into scripting languages for things like Director or Flash because of work projects. I'd always forget what I learned though, because it wasn't my concentration. But now I'm making the jump. I'm excited to connect with a bunch of other smart people who are all going to dig in together.
