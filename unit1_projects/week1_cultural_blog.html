Culture and values.

Q: When you think of the times in your life where you've been the happiest, the proudest, or the most satisfied, which of the following values come to mind?

A:

Accomplishment
Close relationships
Helping other people
Helping society
Independence
Personal development
Self-reliance
Creativity
Inventiveness
Friendships
Humor

Friendships

I went to a show with a group of old friends that I hadn't seen in a long time. I've known them for over 15 years, and while we no longer see each other very often, there was a time when we were together very frequently and were essentially each other's "chosen family". We shared in-jokes from our past, and made plans for future get togethers. It was great to spend time with people who know me in a deep and genuine way. I'm amazed by, and thankful for new friends who are becoming familiar, close, and loved in the same way. Having a sense of "place" and "self" that lives in others helps me feel grounded.

Do I mean it?
